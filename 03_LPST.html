WORK

'Like a Pure Spirit' is an experimental video and book design work that compares the several manipulation scandal in Korea with the characteristic of "Joseon Film", the first movie which came in to Korea by Japanese people. Reality of "Joseon movies", controlled by external power but not known to the public, is seemed not much different from contemporary period. Based on the record about the "Joseon Film", video is methodologically attempted to how our societies are controlled by an inevitable force.





PROCESS

More than 600 papers were scanned to make scenes.
After making the images and let the move, I rendered the whole scene as image files with 12 frames.
Then, all these scenes were scanned with rubbing to the scanner. These process made up unpredictable new images.






BOOK : research and production book


The book includes researches about Joseon Film and production process of 'Like a Pure Spirit'(2017).
This book consists of A-B-A' format.

A : flipping book (printed on semi-transparent paper)
B : research and production book
A' : flipping book
